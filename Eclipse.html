THE SOLAR ECLIPSE!

The solar eclipse this year will occur on August 21, 2017. You need special glasses that reflect the light away from your eyes, yet you can still view the sun. Only during a total solar eclipse, called totality, is when you can view the sun's corona with the naked eye. The sun's corona is the outlining of the sun when the moon coveres it.. That is the point when you do not need to wear any gear, like the kind below.
